Welcome.

This site presents a taste of what the Wikimedia fundraising team has learned over the course of hundreds and hundreds of tests.

On this site, you play a game. First, you'll see two different types of banners. Choose the one you think inspired the most donations, and then you'll go to a page that shows how right (or wrong) you were. Keep going to see more tests.

Please check the about page to learn more about how and why we've run these tests.
